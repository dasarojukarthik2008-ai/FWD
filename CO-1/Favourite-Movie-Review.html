JERSEY

Jersey(Telugu, 2019) is an emotional sports drama starring Nani as Arjun, a talented cricketer whose career ends early due to circumstances and bad decisions. Years later, Arjun lives a quiet and unsuccessful life, burdened by regret and the feeling that he has let himself down. He stays with his wife Sarah (played by Shraddha Srinath) and their young son Kittu, who looks up to his father without knowing his painful past.\

The story takes a turn when Kittu's simple desire—to see his father play cricket—reignites Arjun's lost passion. Despite being in his late thirties and facing health issues, Arjun decides to return to professional cricket. Sarah supports him emotionally but worries about their future, creating a realistic conflict between dreams and responsibilities. Arjun trains hard, fighting age, criticism, and self-doubt.

At its heart, Jersey is not just about cricket but about second chances, self-respect, and a father's love for his son. Arjun's journey shows that success is not always about trophies, but about courage and dignity. The film ends on a deeply emotional note, making Jersey one of the most inspiring and touching Telugu films.
