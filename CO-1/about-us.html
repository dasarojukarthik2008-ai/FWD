KL University

KLH University, officially K L Deemed to be University, is a private higher education institution in India, established in 1980. It was originally founded as K L College of Engineering and later became a deemed university in 2009. The university's main campus is in Vaddeswaram, Andhra Pradesh, with several campuses including its Hyderabad campus for UG and PG courses. KLH offers a wide range of programs in engineering, management, science, technology, and related fields. The university focuses on industry-oriented education, research, and skill development. klh.edu.in KLH has strong industry connections, with many companies participating in its placement drives. Vidyavision It emphasizes academic research and innovation across disciplines. klh.edu.in KLH is recognized by regulatory bodies like the UGC and AICTE and has received various rankings in national frameworks. The Hyderabad campus includes modern facilities and specialized schools such as the Global Business School. The Times of India KLH aims to provide a balanced educational environment, combining academics, practical training, and extracurricular opportunities.
